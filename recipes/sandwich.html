Sandwich Recipe

SANDWICH OF YOUR DREAMS

The ultimate sandwich

  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ham
  • Cheese

Directions:

  1. add one slice of BREAD onto plate
  2. add Ham on top of the Bread
  3. add Cheese on top of the Ham
  4. add Lettuce on top of the Cheese
  5. add Tomato on top of the Lettuce
  6. add another slice of BREAD on top of the tower of ingredients
