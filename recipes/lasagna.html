Lasagna Recipe

The Best Lasagna you will ever eat

This Lasanga will blow your socks off!

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Parmesan Cheese

Directions:

  1. Add Lasagna Sheet to Baking pan
  2. Layer with Marinara Sauce on top of Sheet
  3. Layer Ground Beef on top of Marinara Sauce
  4. Layer Parmesan Cheese on top of Ground Beef
  5. Repeat Steps until desired Lasagna Height
