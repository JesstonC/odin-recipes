Carbonara Recipe

The Best Carbonara

Carbonara with the best flavor in the world

  • Pasta
  • Carbonara Sauce
  • Water
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan Cheese

Directions:

  1. Bring water to a boil and add pasta until cooked
  2. While Pasta cooks, Fry Bacon until crispy, and cut bacon into bits
  3. Strain Pasta and Fry in pan with Carbonara Sauce
  4. Sprinkle Bacon Bits and Parmesan Cheese
